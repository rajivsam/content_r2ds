* Organizations with operational datasets spread across legacy systems and reporting workflows
* Teams making recurring monthly or quarterly planning, forecasting, pricing, or resource decisions
* Leaders who need analytical outputs that are reproducible and understandable across stakeholders

* Operational process and decision-cycle mapping
* Data understanding, profiling, and quality analysis
* Dataset modernization and metadata capture
* Reusable feature engineering and analytical asset development
* Statistical and machine learning model development
* Analytical knowledge preservation for repeatability and handoff

* Predictive and optimization model development
